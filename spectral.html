Empirical Spectral Density (ESD)

ESD represents the distribution of singular values from layer weight matrices. Using the weightwatcher package, we analyzed these values layer-by-layer to understand spectral properties of the network.

Log-Log ESD Dynamics

This video visualizes how the ESD evolves during training. Power law behavior (visible as linearity in the log-log spectrum) is a sign of effective generalization.
